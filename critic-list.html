The Critic List

a running list of restaurants — tried, loved, and still craved

what it is

A personal restaurant tracker: one running list of the places I've tried, the places I loved, and the places I'm still saving up a craving for.

the problem

Restaurant recommendations go to the notes app to die. A name with no context — who told you about it, whether you ever went, whether it was worth it — is barely better than nothing. And when someone finally asks "where should we eat?", the answer is somewhere in a graveyard of screenshots.

how it works

Add a place, mark it tried, loved, or wishlist, and jot the one-line verdict while you still remember it. Over time it becomes the thing you actually wanted all along: your own little restaurant canon, in your own words.
