Lindsey Noelle LePlae

bio

Lindsey was born in Los Angeles and grew up moving a lot. She's lived in New York, Los Angeles, Tokyo, and Berlin, and currently lives in San Francisco.

These days she spends her free time building apps and websites with AI, and thinking about how humanity and AI come together. She's interested in art and fashion — she goes to museums the way other people go to the movies, reads with a book club in Oakland, and once crossed the country by train. For the fuller map of what she's into, wander the digital garden.

Lindsey received her BA in art history from NYU and her JD from USC. Before law school, she worked at a major art museum in New York. She's currently a practicing attorney.
